Rule Name

 WTR 7.1.19 (d) - Storage – Fencing Enclosure

Rule Description

7.1.19   For water tanks in developments other than landed residential premises:


Where a fencing enclosure is used to house the water tanks and/or its ancillary equipment, the material for the fencing enclosure shall be of welded-mesh or expanded-mesh with mesh openings no larger than 75 mm (height) x 25 mm (width). When there is no ceiling, the height of the fencing enclosure shall not be less than 2.5 m above the highest ground level and shall be reinforced with 300 mm outriggers. Where a ceiling exists over the enclosure, the enclosure may extend from floor to ceiling without any outriggers.
