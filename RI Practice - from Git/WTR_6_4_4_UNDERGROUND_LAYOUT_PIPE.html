Rule Name

 WTR 6.4.4 - Underground Layout Pipe Cover Requirement

Rule Description

 Piping laid underground outside buildings should have a depth of not less than 500 mm of cover measured from top of the pipe to the finished surface of the ground to prevent damage to the pipes.
